Smart Learning Edge

Smart Books - Augmented Reality enabled

My Bright Book Series- The My Bright Books Series, perhaps the first of its kind in the country, delivers rich interactive content focused on curriculum-based education, learning and an “anytime, anywhere teacher” for young children. The content tailored for preschool children aged between 2.5 years to 6 years, provides creative and engaging learning material. These books are enabled with AR- Augmented Reality technology which adds magic to learning.

With the My Bright Books Series, as AR which is enabled on each page and almost on each content on the pages, it allows for learning beyond the classroom providing a teacher-like experience, guidance and assistance even at home.

Bright Concept Teacher - Coview and Teach

With the Bright Concept Teacher you will never feel the scarcity of a highly experienced and competent teacher in the classroom. There are more than 150 concept videos for each level of the preschool- Playgroup, Nursery, LKG and UKG. These concept videos are content rich and visually engaging in a manner that is developed to assist and supplement the classroom teacher, enabling her to inspire and lead effective.

Brilla- The Multiple Intelligence

This is a mobile Learning App fro children that contributes to a child’s overall personality and intelligence development potential in their homes. Each child has their own learning style, multiple intelligences define how children learn using various approached. The app has 100+ activities for the child to do based on the 4 intelligence, these are- My Bright Brain, Be Bright, My Anytime, Anywhere Teacher and Be Social.

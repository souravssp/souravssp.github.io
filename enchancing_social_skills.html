Social Skills Development for Bright Kids at home

Preschool years are magic years in your child’s development!

A healthy social development will contribute to the holistic development of your child’s cognitive abilities, emotional expression and overall growth.

Our curriculum, based on 10 plus years of experience and practice is designed to facilitate the social development of children as we believe that social skills can help your child:

  Develop language skills

  Develop communication skills

  Build self-confidence

  Strengthen learning skills

  Build GK & awareness of the Environment

And for fostering these developments we have developed the Activity Calendar- for all around the year listing various activities.

The Activity Calendar lists the various activities based on different themes of the month for the entire year. During the month, activities for your child such as:

  Show-n-Tell

  Role-play

  Festival Celebrations

  Birthday Celebrations

  Circle-Time Talk

All of these are based on various themes of the month. Be it the Show-n-Tell “Rainbow Colours”, Diwali Fashion Parade or Knowledge Buzz- activities are an integral part of your child’s overall development. Encouraging the child to participate in all such activities either with the family and freinds or in the community, is known for making children confident, outgoing, curious and interactive!

Welcome to BrightKid@Home Program

Welcome to Bright Kid @ Home program, a unique Early Childhood Preschool program at the safety and comfort of the home offered by Bright Kid Montessori House who has 13+ years of experience in operating preschools across the country and has developed 25000+ children. About Us

Bright Kid Montessori House was born in April 2007, with a strong conviction to develop children using scientific Montessori methodology, blended with modern multimedia and conventional methods of classroom teaching. Since our humble beginning we have grown into a much bigger family consisting of thousands of children. Our blended methodology of developing children has been extremely successful and has been well proven. Our team consists of well trained Montessorians, experienced and motivated teachers who work under the guidance of few highly accomplished management professionals having credentials from IITs and IIMs.

About Us

Bright Kid Montessori House was founded in 2007 and is associated with providing excellent Montessori based education blended with traditional methods of classroom learning and integrated with the best of education technology. Our blended methodology of developing children at our preschools has been extremely successful and well proven having more than 25,000 students graduating with us over the past decade. Our team comprises well-trained Montessorians, experienced and motivated teachers and attendants who work under the guidance of highly accomplished management professionals having credentials from IITs and IIMs. We are committed in providing the best-of-class, warm, nurturing, well ventilated, safe and secure environment for children.

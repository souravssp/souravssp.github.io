Montessori Learning Edge

Benefits of Montessori Methodology

Focuses on key Developmental Stages

A Montessori Curriculum focuses on key develop-mental milestones in children between the ages of one and half and six-years old.

Encourages Co-Operative play

Children in Montessori classrooms, by the very nature of the environment, learn to respect one another and build a sense of community

Learning is Child-Centered

Montessori preschool students enjoy a classroom and curriculum designed around their specific needs and abilities that allows them to explore and learn at their own pace and on their own terms.

Children Naturally Learn Self-Discipline

The environment naturally teaches children self discipline, and it refines important skills like concentration , self-control and motivation.



Classroom Environment Teaches Order

The sense of order helps facilitate the learning process, teaches self-discipline, and caters to a young child’s innate need for an orderly environment.

Montessori Trained Teachers

Teachers in the Montessori classrooms are “guides”. They are there to facilitate the learning experience.
